Frequently asked questions

How is this different from Chess.com Insights?
Chess.com Insights is great for individual players, but it doesn't let you manage multiple students. Insights64 gives you a multi-student dashboard, weakness detection across your roster, and progress reports you can share with parents — features Chess.com doesn't offer.
Do my students need to do anything?
No. You just need their Chess.com or Lichess username. Both platforms have public game data, so we can analyze their games without any action from them. They don't need to create an account or install anything.
Can I try it free?
Yes! The Player tier is free forever. You can analyze your own games and see how Insights64 works before upgrading to Coach or Academy for student management features.
How long does analysis take?
Initial analysis of 100 games takes about 30-60 minutes. After that, new games are analyzed automatically within hours of being played.
